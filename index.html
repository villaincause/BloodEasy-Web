Blood donation is a life-saving act that directly saves lives, promotes community support, and offers health benefits to donors.

With no substitute for human blood, each donation is invaluable, empowering individuals to make a direct impact on the well-being of others and contributing to a culture of giving.

Blood is the life force that runs through our veins, delivering oxygen from our lungs to every part of our bodies.
Any disruption in this vital process can put a life at risk. In cases of severe accidents,
for instance, victims often succumb to excessive blood loss or brain haemorrhage.


About Us

We are called BloodEasy
BloodEasy was developed for the Regional Center for
Blood Donation and Blood Treatment in Bangladesh.
The goal of us is to make blood donations easier,
more accessible & finding donors, blood banks easier,
which in turn was expected to increase
the amount of donated blood & finding it.
